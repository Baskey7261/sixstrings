Welcome.

At Musical Six Strings, we specialize in curriculum based guitar tuition, which encompasses the Trinity college of London's Classical & Plectrum grade initial through to grade 8 syllabus (2015-2019).

Having a Trinity grade certificate verifies that you have met the criteria required by the Regulated Qualifications Framework (RQF). Credentials such as these are recognized around the globe as the industry standard in the music and entertainments industry.

The grading system begins at grade initial for beginners and continues through to grade 8. Every pupil at Musical Six Strings will undergo an initial assessment to determine their current grade level in accordance with the Trinity College of London (TCL) syllabus.

Grades 6-8 are a minimum requirement for students hoping to be put forward for further education such as a diploma or higher. we encourage all of our pupils to work toward completing each grade but we also cater for pupils that are just aiming to hone there current skills and expand on their musical repertoire.

Our syllabuses:

We run two main syllabuses, Classical and Rock School.
The Classical syllabus is designed around compositions and techniques from the renaissance period up to the present day.
The Rock School syllabus is designed around acoustic and electric guitar, incorporating a wide range of genres and styles suited for both instruments.

CLASSICAL

Designed around the Trinity college of London's Classical & Plectrum grade initial through to grade 8 syllabus.


From initial to grade eight, the Classical Syllabus covers a wide range of repertoire from all genres of Classical music. The technical work includes uniquely composed exercises to ensure the required technique is in place to complete each grade. Students will be trained through the early grades with exercises designed to build on the skill level and technical ability which will be heavily relied upon during the later grades.

ROCK SCHOOL

Designed around the Trinity college of London's Rock and Pop grade initial through to grade 8 syllabus.


Designed to work alongside the Trinity College of London's Rock and Pop Syllabus, The Rock school syllabus focuses on merging the techniques required for both acoustic and electric guitar, with a view to building a skill set that will be useful in building a repertoire that suits both instruments, with an emphasis on a wide range of finger-styles and plectrum technique. Students will be encouraged to explore a range of styles including, rock and pop, jazz, blues and country. The syllabus covers chord progressions and patterns, along with standard and none standard scales and arpeggios, using both plectrum and fingers.
